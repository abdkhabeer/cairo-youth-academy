Why Cairo?

A Once-in-a-Lifetime
Journey of Knowledge

Egypt has been the beating heart of Islamic scholarship for over a thousand years. Our program places your family in that living tradition, studying Qur'an under qualified huffaz, building Arabic fluency through daily immersion, and experiencing Muslim community life in one of the world's great cities.

Every detail, from flights to food, is handled so families can focus on the experience, not the logistics.
